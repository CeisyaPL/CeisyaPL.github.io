CSV Format Instructions:

Your CSV file must have these columns in order:

type,question,option1,option2,option3,option4,correct_answer
Question Types:
  1. multiple_choice: All options and correct_answer required 
    multiple_choice,What is 2+2?,1,2,3,4,4
  2. fill_blank: Leave options empty, provide correct_answer 
    fill_blank,The capital of France is _____,,,,,Paris
  3. open_ended: Leave options empty, provide keywords in correct_answer 
    open_ended,Explain how photosynthesis works,,,,,sunlight,chlorophyll,plants,energy,glucose
    For open-ended questions, separate keywords with commas. The answer is considered correct if it contains 70% of the keywords.
Example CSV Content:
type,question,option1,option2,option3,option4,correct_answer
multiple_choice,What is 2+2?,1,2,3,4,4
fill_blank,The capital of France is _____,,,,,Paris
multiple_choice,Who is CEO of Tesla?,Bill Gates,Elon Musk,Jeff Bezos,Tim Cook,Elon Musk
open_ended,Explain how photosynthesis works,,,,,sunlight,chlorophyll,plants,energy,glucose
